Got this in a text or email? You can't scan your own screen — just tap the link above (no scanning needed), then follow the steps below to keep it on your home screen.

📱 iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open the link in Safari (not Chrome).
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an up‑arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add — the icon appears on your home screen.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open the link in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top‑right).
  3. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).
  4. Tap Add / Install.

After opening it, tap “⬇ Save for offline use” on the start screen — then it works without a signal on the battlefield (GPS still needs location on).
